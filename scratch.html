Photography is an art,
and those who truly appreciate it are an endangered species.

To be able to capture how you see the word and give it unto others is a greater form of empathy than words can convey.

  1. Photos are useful!
    • They help you learn about things.
    • They help you tell stories.
    • They help you share your culture!
  2. Photos are fun!
    • They help you make friends.
    • They help you learn new, cool skills.
    • They make boring things less boring!
  3. Photos are time capsules!
    • They show the history of the world.
    • They can show how you were when you were young.
    • They can show what your life was like before it all went downhill after you went to college.
